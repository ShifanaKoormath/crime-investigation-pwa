3. Fraud Report
Incident Type: Online Fraud
Date: February 15, 2025
Time: 2:00 PM
Location: Online (Fake E-commerce Website)
Victim: Ms. Anjali Menon
Description:
Ms. Anjali Menon reported being scammed by a fraudulent e-commerce website. She purchased a mobile phone worth ₹15,000, but never received the product. The website is no longer accessible, and her payment was made via UPI transfer. Cybercrime authorities are investigating the website’s operators.
